A Little About Me


My Career Path + Trajectory


I'm a strategic HR Business Partner with experience as a Generalist. I plan to continue on this path until someday I become a HR Director, VP, or even CHRO. I'm also open to the idea of becoming a Specialist later in my career, focusing on a core HR function such as L&D or Talent.

My HR Experience


I've worked for several different companies, including: Amazon, doTERRA, Swire Coca-Cola, PillPack, Enterprise Rent-A-Car, TaxHawk, and Chick-fil-A. By working in companies from a variety of industries and sizes, I've learned how to scale up big as well as how to build from scratch.

My Future Contributions


Aside from having a successful career in HR, I honestly hope to become a professor someday. I feel very strongly about giving back, and there's no better way to do this than by teaching and mentoring the next generation of young professionals. I hope to make a difference by giving others access to opportunities that I never had.
